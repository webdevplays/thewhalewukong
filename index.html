The Whale Wukong – What to Expect?

Whale Wukong, developed by Game Science, transports players into the legendary world of Chinese mythology, where ancient gods, mythological creatures, and legendary battles await. Inspired by the classic tale Journey to the West, this action RPG gives you control of the iconic Monkey King, Sun Wukong, as he embarks on an epic journey filled with danger, wonder, and intense combat.

The game presents a visually stunning world with intricate details that bring Chinese folklore to life. Whether you’re battling fierce demons, solving puzzles, or uncovering Wukong’s deeper purpose, Whale Wukong offers an immersive experience with rich storytelling and a deep sense of wonder.

With its fast-paced, dynamic combat system, Whale Wukong allows players to utilize Sun Wukong’s transformation abilities and his iconic staff to defeat enemies in countless creative ways. The game offers a variety of playstyles, ensuring that no two encounters feel the same.
